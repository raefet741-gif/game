# Clash Night — Truth or Dare Bank

180 prompts total: **130 truths** across 10 categories, plus **50 dares**.
Party-friendly, works for any group. Mix light and deep for the best pace.

---

## Truths

### Relationships & love (1–15)

1. What's the most romantic thing you've ever done for someone?
2. Have you ever loved someone who didn't love you back?
3. What's the longest you've had a crush on someone without telling them?
4. Have you ever gotten back with an ex? Would you again?
5. What's the pettiest reason you've ended (or wanted to end) a relationship?
6. Have you ever lied about your feelings to protect someone?
7. What's a small thing someone did that made you fall for them?
8. Do you believe in love at first sight, or is that a myth?
9. What's the boldest move you've ever made on someone you liked?
10. Have you ever been in two situationships at the same time?
11. What's the most jealous you've ever been?
12. Would you rather be the one who loves more, or the one who's loved more?
13. What's a relationship habit of yours that you're not proud of?
14. Have you ever stayed in something way too long? Why?
15. What's the nicest compliment you've ever received from a partner or crush?

### Your type & attraction (16–27)

16. Describe your type in exactly three words.
17. Looks, humor, or intelligence — rank them and be honest.
18. What's an unusual thing you find attractive that others might not?
19. Who's a celebrity that's 100% your type?
20. What's the first thing you notice about someone?
21. Have you ever changed your "type" completely? What caused it?
22. Voice, smile, or eyes — what wins you over fastest?
23. What's a "green flag" that instantly makes you more interested?
24. Confident and loud, or quiet and mysterious?
25. What's the most superficial thing you care about that you wish you didn't?
26. Would you date someone with the exact same personality as you?
27. What trait do you find attractive now that you didn't care about at 16?

### Red flags & dealbreakers (28–39)

28. What's your absolute number-one dealbreaker?
29. What's a red flag you've ignored before, and regretted?
30. Name a red flag you think most people underrate.
31. Is there a red flag you find weirdly attractive?
32. What's a habit in others that instantly turns you off?
33. Bad texter or always on their phone — which is worse?
34. What's a red flag *you* have that you'll admit to right now?
35. Would you rather date someone messy or someone controlling?
36. What's the fastest a date has gone wrong for you?
37. What excuse have you used to get out of a second date?
38. What's a green flag that outweighs almost any red flag?
39. Rude to waiters, or never says thank you — dealbreaker or not?

### Friends & friendship (40–51)

40. Who in this room have you known the longest, and how did you meet?
41. What's the nicest thing a friend has ever done for you?
42. Have you ever kept a secret from a close friend? Do they know?

48. What's a quality you look for in a best friend?
49. Have you ever had a falling-out and later made up? What happened?
50. Who's the funniest person in this room? No fence-sitting.
51. What's a secret talent of yours your friends don't know about?

### Things you hate & pet peeves (62–71)

### What you really want (72–83)

72. What do you want more than anything right now?
73. If money weren't a factor, what would you be doing with your life?
74. What's something you want but feel embarrassed to admit?

### Dreams & ambitions (84–95)

96. What's the biggest secret you're comfortable sharing right now?

## Dares

### Funny & performance (1–15)

1. Speak in a fake accent until it's your turn again.
2. Do your best impression of someone in this room — let us guess who.
3. Sing the chorus of the last song you listened to, out loud.
4. Do a dramatic runway walk across the room.
5. Talk in rhymes for the next two rounds.
6. Do your best slow-motion action-movie scene.
7. Act out your morning routine in complete silence.
8. Give a passionate 30-second speech about something boring (e.g. socks).
9. Do an over-the-top fashion commentary on everyone's outfit.
10. Perform a made-up dance and name it.
11. Narrate everything you do like a nature documentary for one minute.
12. Do your best evil-villain laugh — commit fully.
13. Pretend to be a news anchor reporting on this party.
14. Sing your next three sentences like an opera singer.
15. Do an impression of a baby learning to walk.

### Social & phone (16–27)

16. Show the last photo in your camera roll.
17. Post the most recent selfie in your phone as your story — no edits.
18. Let the person on your left write your next text (to someone safe).
19. Read your last five sent messages out loud.
20. Show your most-used emoji and explain it.
21. Text a friend "I need to tell you something" and don't explain for 10 minutes.
22. Call a random contact and sing them "Happy Birthday."
23. Show the last thing you searched online.
24. Let the group pick a new (temporary) profile picture for you.
25. Read out your most recent notification, whatever it is.
26. Show your screen time for today and take the roast.
27. Voice-note a compliment to the last person you texted.

### Physical & silly (28–39)

28. Do 15 pushups (or 15 squats) while telling a joke.
29. Balance a spoon on your nose for 20 seconds.
30. Hold a plank while everyone counts down from 30.
31. Try to lick your elbow — prove it's impossible.
32. Do your best cartwheel or the safest version you can manage.
33. Spin around 10 times, then walk a straight line.
34. Do the worm, or your best attempt at it.
35. Keep a straight face while the group tries to make you laugh for 30 seconds.
36. Do an impression of a robot running out of battery.
37. Wear your jacket or shirt inside out for the next three rounds.
38. Do 10 jumping jacks while singing the alphabet.
39. Freeze like a statue whenever someone claps, for two rounds.

### Group & interactive (40–50)

40. Let the group ask you three rapid-fire questions — answer instantly.
41. Swap one item of clothing with the person on your right (a hat, jacket, etc.).
42. Give a genuine compliment to every person in the room.
43. Let the opposing team choose your next dare.
44. Do a two-person mirror challenge with someone the group picks.
45. Whisper a secret to one person — they decide whether to reveal it.
46. Team up with someone and act out a famous movie scene.
47. Let someone feed you a snack while you're blindfolded and guess what it is.
48. Have a 20-second staring contest with the person across from you.
49. Do a duet lip-sync with a partner the group chooses.
50. Recreate a group photo pose the room designs for you.

---

### How to use with scoring

- **Truth answered honestly:** +1 point (crowd decides if it counts).
- **Dare completed fully:** +2 points.
- **Chicken out:** opposing team steals 1 point.
- **Bluff bonus:** for the "guess my truth" variant, +1 for each player fooled.

Mix categories as you draw so the night swings between funny, spicy, and deep.
